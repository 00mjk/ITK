

Follow these steps to:
1) Subscribe to the Insight users mailing list.
http://www.itk.org/mailman/listinfo/insight-users


2) Get a copy of cvs
http://www.cvshome.org


3) Download CMake
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@www.cmake.org:/cvsroot/CMake login
(respond with password cmake)
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@www.cmake.org:/cvsroot/CMake co CMake


4) Build CMake


mkdir CMake-myarch
cd CMake-myarch
setenv CC your c-compiler
setenv CXX your c++-compiler
../CMake/configure
gmake
gmake


4) Download Dart
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@public.kitware.com:/Dart/cvsroot login
(respond with password dart)
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@public.kitware.com:/Dart/cvsroot co Dart


5) Download Insight


a) Download tarball
b) Checkout a version from the cvs repository
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@www.itk.org:/insight/cvsroot login
Password is insight
cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@www.itk.org:/insight/cvsroot co Insight


setenv CC your c-compiler
setenv CXX your c++-compiler
mkdir Insight-myarch
cd Insight-myarch
../CMake-myarch/Source/cmake  ../Insight
